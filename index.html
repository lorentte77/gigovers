What we want to do
We believe that the Internet Computer is the best ecosystem for building a metaverse. Technologically it’s ready to host the whole world on the blockchain: the game, the assets, the players. Working on this belief, we want to bring a metaverse to the IC.
We’ve started with a collection of ICPunks and Infinity Frogs. Our next goal is to create a bridge from Ethereum to IC, so that people can freely move NFTs (also Decentraland assets) to the IC and back.
The next step would be to establish a DAO to fuel the growth of the crosschain ecosystem of apps and tools.
We believe in the open gasless NFT ecosystem.
